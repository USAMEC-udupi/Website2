1950: The Humble Beginnings

Founded by Swamiji in a small village with just a thatched hut and a vision, USAMEC began as a spiritual center offering free education to local children. The first students were taught under a banyan tree, with lessons combining traditional scriptures with modern subjects. This humble beginning laid the foundation for what would become a nationwide network of educational and spiritual institutions.

Our Institutions

32+ institutions across India serving diverse communities through education and spiritual guidance

Divine Light Gurukul

Established: 1955 | Varanasi, UP

Our first residential school combining modern education with traditional gurukul values. Offers CBSE curriculum with special emphasis on Sanskrit and Indian philosophy.

Divine Light College

Established: 1971 | Bangalore, KA

Premier degree college offering Arts, Science and Commerce programs with value-added courses in meditation and yoga. NAAC 'A' Grade accredited institution.

Divine Light Ashram

Established: 1962 | Rishikesh, UK

Spiritual retreat center offering courses in meditation, yoga and vedic studies. Hosts annual international spiritual conference attracting seekers worldwide.
